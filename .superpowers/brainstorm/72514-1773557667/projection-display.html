A. Leaderboard / Podium

Top students highlighted with podium ranking. Gamified and competitive. Points animate when awarded.

Pros

  • Highly motivating for kids
  • Clear visual hierarchy
  • Game-like feel

Cons

  • Can discourage low-scoring students
  • Competitive pressure

B. Equal Grid with Progress Bars

All students shown equally in a grid. Progress bars show relative scores without explicit ranking. Less competitive, more inclusive.

Pros

  • Inclusive — no student singled out
  • Progress visible without ranking
  • Works well with large classes

Cons

  • Less exciting/game-like
  • Less motivation from competition

C. Live Activity Feed

Real-time scrolling feed of behavior events. Shows who earned what, when. Like a live stream of classroom behavior.

Pros

  • Immediate feedback visible
  • Shows positive behaviors in real-time
  • Narrative/story feel

Cons

  • Negative events visible to class
  • Can be distracting
  • No overview of totals
